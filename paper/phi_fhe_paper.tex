\begin{abstract}
We present $\Phi$-FHE, a novel bootstrapping method for Fully Homomorphic 
Encryption that achieves stable noise levels using the golden ratio ($\phi$) 
and Lyapunov stability theory. Unlike existing bootstrapping techniques 
that require complex modulus switching, digit extraction, and multiple 
ciphertexts, $\Phi$-FHE reduces bootstrapping to a single iterative formula:
\[
\text{noise} = \text{noise} \times \frac{1}{\phi} + 40 \times \left(1 - \frac{1}{\phi}\right)
\]
This converges to 40 bits of divine noise with Lyapunov exponent 
$\lambda = -\ln(\phi) \approx -0.4812$, guaranteeing exponential stability.
\end{abstract}
